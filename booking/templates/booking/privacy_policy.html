Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to our Bus Reservation system. We highly value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal data. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our services:

  • Personal Information (Name, Email, Phone Number, Payment Details)
  • Travel Preferences (Destinations, Seat Selection, Travel History)
  • Technical Data (IP Address, Device Type, Browser Type, Cookies)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Process your bookings and payments securely
  • Provide customer support and service updates
  • Enhance user experience and website functionality
  • Send promotional offers (only if you opt-in)

4. Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your personal data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

We may share limited data with third-party payment gateways for secure transactions. We do not sell or trade your personal information.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data
  • Opt-out of promotional emails
  • Request data portability

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be notified via email or posted on our website.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this policy, please contact us at support@busreservation.com
