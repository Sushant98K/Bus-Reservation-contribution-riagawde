Terms of Service

  • Users must provide accurate personal details while booking tickets.

2. Payment & Refund Policy

  • Tickets are non-refundable after confirmation unless stated otherwise.

5. Luggage Policy

  • Each passenger is allowed one carry-on bag and one checked-in bag (weight limits apply).
  • Oversized or additional luggage may be subject to extra charges.
  • The company is not responsible for lost, stolen, or misplaced items.

6. Code of Conduct

  • Passengers must follow the instructions of the driver and staff at all times.
  • Unruly behavior, harassment, or disturbances to other passengers will lead to removal from the bus without refund.
  • Smoking and consumption of tobacco products are strictly prohibited on board.

7. Privacy & Data Protection

  • Your personal details will be used solely for ticket bookings and customer service purposes.
